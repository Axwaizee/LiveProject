FAQs

What dance styles do you offer?

We offer classes in Bollywood and Hip Hop dance styles.

How can I register for classes?

To register for classes, please fill out the registration form on our website or contact us directly.

What are the payment options?

We accept payments through online transfer, credit/debit cards, and cash payments at our studio.

What are the age groups for your dance classes?

We offer dance classes for various age groups, including children, teenagers, and adults. Our classes are tailored to suit the specific needs and abilities of each age group.

Do I need any prior dance experience to join your classes?

No prior dance experience is necessary to join our classes. Whether you're a complete beginner or have some dance background, our instructors will guide you through the learning process at your own pace.

What should I wear to the dance classes?

We recommend wearing comfortable clothing that allows for ease of movement, such as leggings, t-shirts, or tank tops. For footwear, sneakers or dance shoes are suitable depending on the dance style.

Are there any performance opportunities for students?

Yes, we provide performance opportunities for our students throughout the year. These may include recitals, showcases, competitions, or community events where students can showcase their skills and talents.
